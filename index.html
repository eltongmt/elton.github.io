Hi! I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a BS in Psychology and a minor in Computer Science.

I completed my honors thesis in the Developing Intelligence Lab under Chen Yu, where I am currently a lab technician. My work in the lab has focused on using machine learning and deep learning to model/categorize natural-behavior data.

Apart from research, I have greatly supplemented my education in machine learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, and robotics through e-learning.

Broadly, I am interested in embodied AI as a way for artificial agents to learn in a more generalizable/grounded way.
